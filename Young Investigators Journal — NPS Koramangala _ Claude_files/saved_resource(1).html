About the Journal

Where curiosity becomes discovery.

The Young Investigators Journal at NPS Koramangala is a student-led, peer-reviewed platform that celebrates curiosity, critical thinking, and original research.

Over a structured 12-week journey, students explore questions, design investigations, analyse results, and present their findings in a formal research format. This journal is a space where learning moves beyond textbooks — and into discovery.

Our Process

From question to publication

Every paper in this journal follows a rigorous 12-week research journey, modelled on real academic practice.

01

Question

Students identify a genuine research question from any discipline.

02

Investigation

A structured methodology is designed and applied with faculty guidance.

03

Analysis

Data and findings are rigorously analysed and interpreted.

04

Peer Review

Submissions are reviewed by fellow student editors and faculty advisors.

05

Publication

Accepted papers are formally typeset and published in the journal.
