About the Program

Where curiosity becomes discovery.

Catalyst is a 12-week structured student research program at NPS Koramangala. Students move through every stage of real scientific inquiry — from forming a question to presenting findings.

Each student designs and conducts their own experiment, collects and analyses data, writes a formal research paper, and presents their work. No templates. No shortcuts. Real research.

Outcomes

What Students Will Achieve

01
🔬

Real Experiment with Data

A hands-on experiment designed and conducted entirely by the student, with documented methodology and raw data.

02
📊

Graphs & Analysis

Data visualised through charts, with written interpretation of findings, patterns, and trends.

03
📄

Research Paper

A formally written, mentor-reviewed paper following standard academic structure — introduction through references.

04
🎤

Final Presentation

A structured, confident oral presentation to a live audience — building academic communication skills.

05
🏅

Published Researcher

Accepted papers published in the Catalyst journal — a formal academic credential for each student author.
